🔍 Why CPNs Work

There are 117,740,604 invalid SSNs available
These are combinations that:
  • Are formatted like real SSNs (AAA-GG-SSSS)
  • Are not assigned or are structurally invalid by SSA rules
  • Will never conflict with real SSNs in government databases
✅ This gives CPN creators a massive pool of identifiers — without overlapping real SSNs.

📌 SSN System At-A-Glance

  • Total valid SSNs: ~889 million
  • Issued from 1936 to 2024: ~500 million
  • Unused: ~389 million (still untouched)
  • At current rate, we're good until ~2088

🧠 Built for Bulk Generation & Testing

By sticking to the 117M+ known invalid SSNs, CPN Creator:

  • ✔ Avoids overlapping with real issued SSNs
  • ✔ Reduces the risk of identity flagging or fraud triggers
  • ✔ Can produce millions of identifiers before any reuse

Ideal for sandbox testing, automation, and education use cases.

✅ SSA Post-2011 Rules

  • Area number (first 3 digits) cannot be 000, 666, or 900–999
  • Group number (middle 2 digits) cannot be 00
  • Serial number (last 4 digits) cannot be 0000

CPN Creator respects all these rules when generating identifiers.

👥 Why This Matters

With only ~335 million people in the U.S. (as of 2024), and 889 million valid SSNs in the system, there’s still over 389 million available combinations left.
